- The dance world is full ofstyles, people, and ideas-

Dance styles each have their own stories, competitions, rules, and variations.
Breakdance began with hip hop!
Ballet began with court dances for a king who liked dance.
However, it's more complex than that!

Dancers can do a wide range of things, like content creation, competitions, and taking classes at studios

- Dancers will work very hard to perfect their craft and make themselves stronger, faster, and cooler

A dancer will learn new skills from other styles, and learn choreography from other dancers to improve.
Breakdancers will use acrobatic skills and jazz dancers will get groovy in their own way.
both styles require high energy, confidence, and willingness to perform and not just move!

- Most dancers will develop their ownstyle through improvisation
